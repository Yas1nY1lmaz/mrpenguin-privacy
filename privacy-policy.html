Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Mr.Penguin handles user data.

Information We Collect

Our app may collect the following data:

  • Device information for ad serving (via Google AdMob)
  • In-game progress data (stored locally on device only)

Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data to serve interest-based ads. You can review Google's privacy policy here.

Third-Party Services

This app only uses Google AdMob. No data is shared with any other third party.

Children's Privacy

Mr.Penguin is a fun mobile game suitable for players of all ages. We do not collect any personal information from any user, including children. Everyone can play safely.

Data Security

In-game progress data (gold, score, etc.) is stored only on the user's device and is never transmitted to our servers.
